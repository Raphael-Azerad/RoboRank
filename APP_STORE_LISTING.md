## 📝 Description (App Store, max 4000 chars)

**RoboRank is the analytics platform built for VEX V5 Robotics teams who want to win.**

Whether you're heading into a qualifier or grinding toward Worlds, RoboRank gives you the scouting intel, performance data, and match predictions you need to make smarter decisions on competition day.

**WHAT YOU GET:**

🎯 **RoboRank Score** — Our proprietary 0-100 rating combines win rate, OPR, schedule strength, and skills performance into a single number that actually means something.

📊 **Auto-Generated Scouting Reports** — One tap and you get a full CSV/Excel breakdown of every team at any event. No more spreadsheet heroics at 11 PM the night before a tournament.

⚔️ **Match Predictor** — Pick four teams, simulate the alliance, see the win probability. Powered by a weighted sigmoid model trained on real match data.

🏆 **Tournament Tools** — Live brackets, qualification rankings, schedule difficulty scores, and elimination visualizations.

📝 **Team Notes** — Tag teams, categorize observations, pin strategy notes — all synced across your roster.

📈 **Season Progress** — Compare up to four teams across multiple seasons to see who's actually improving.

🔔 **Smart Notifications** — Get alerts when watched events post matches, when your team's ranking changes, or when a partner team wins an award.

**WHY ROBORANK:**

✓ Free for every team, every user — no paywalls, no limits
✓ Real-time data from the official RobotEvents API
✓ Designed mobile-first for the pit and the stands
✓ Built by people who actually compete in VEX

**PERFECT FOR:**
• Drive teams scouting opponents
• Captains running alliance selection
• Coaches tracking season-long progress
• Parents and fans following a team

Download RoboRank and walk into your next event with answers, not guesses.
